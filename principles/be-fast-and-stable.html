Be fast and stable

Lighthouse 'performance' dimension, expressed as a principle. The page should load quickly and stay visually stable: good Core Web Vitals (LCP, INP, CLS), a light main thread, and no layout jank from late-arriving content. Speed and stability are part of modern UX, not separate from it.

Applicability: Applies to every site: load quickly and stay visually stable; good Core Web Vitals are a baseline expectation.

Required coverage: all 5 checks defined in the vendored principles.json, with explicit pass, issues, N/A, blocked, or not-run evidence per site.
