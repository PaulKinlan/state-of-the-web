Principle result breakdown

Be trustworthy

The site is honest and humane. No dark patterns (confirmshaming, forced continuity, disguised ads, nagging consent walls), honest defaults, clear pricing and consent, easy reversal of actions, predictable controls, and humane error handling that prevents and recovers from mistakes rather than blaming the user. Covers the Nielsen heuristics and WCAG-Understandable criteria (error prevention, recovery, user control, consistency) and the humane-forms guides; distinct from maximize-content-reduce-noise, which bans the FORM of interruption but not the deceptive INTENT.

Applicability and measurement

Applicability: Applies to every site: be honest and humane. No dark patterns, honest defaults, easy reversal, and humane error handling that prevents and recovers from mistakes.

Required coverage: all 4 checks defined in the vendored principles.json, with explicit pass, issues, N/A, blocked, or not-run evidence per site.
