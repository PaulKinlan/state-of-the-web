Principle result breakdown

Be sustainable

The site is resource-efficient. It respects a page-weight budget appropriate to its purpose, ships optimised assets at appropriate resolutions, avoids wasteful background work and over-fetching, and prefers the lightest technique that achieves the result. Overlaps performance on outcomes (lighter is faster) but its intent (resource/carbon efficiency) is distinct, so the auditor weighs asset weight and wasted work, not just perceived speed. Backed by the W3C Web Sustainability Guidelines.

Applicability and measurement

Applicability: Applies to every site as a lens on resource efficiency, but is judged with proportion: a media-heavy product (video platform, game) legitimately ships more bytes than a text site.

Required coverage: all 3 checks defined in the vendored principles.json, with explicit pass, issues, N/A, blocked, or not-run evidence per site.
