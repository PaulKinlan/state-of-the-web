Principle result breakdown

Provide guided navigation

Help users understand where they are and guide their attention through the experience, using state-aware UI and positioning rather than leaving them to orient themselves.

Applicability and measurement

Applicability: Applies to every site with more than a single static view: help users understand where they are and guide attention.

Required coverage: all 3 checks defined in the vendored principles.json, with explicit pass, issues, N/A, blocked, or not-run evidence per site.
